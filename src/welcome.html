RecapTube AI

AI summaries, translation and auto-chapters for any YouTube video.

✨ What it does

📝
Summary

A faithful summary plus key takeaways, generated from the transcript.

🌍
Translation

The summary is written directly in your browser's language — any source language.

📑
Auto chapters

Topic chapters with timestamps when the creator didn't add any.

🎯
Jump to topic

Click a chapter (or a marker on the progress bar) to seek the video.

🚀 Get started

1
Click the RecapTube icon and pick a provider — Anthropic Claude or OpenAI GPT.
2
Paste your API key and press Save. Get one from Anthropic or OpenAI.
3
Open any YouTube video with subtitles. The panel appears next to the video with the summary and chapters.

💡 Good to know

  • RecapTube needs a video that has a transcript/subtitles available.
  • Results are cached per video & language for 30 days — reopening a video is instant and free.
  • Change the output language, summary length and toggles anytime in the popup.
  • Exclude specific channels from the popup if you don't want them processed.
