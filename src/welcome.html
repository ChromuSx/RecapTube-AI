RecapTubeAI

AI summaries, translation & auto-chapters for any YouTube video.

auto_awesome What it does

📝
Summary

A faithful summary plus key takeaways, from the transcript.

🌍
Translation

The summary is written directly in your browser's language.

📑
Auto chapters

Topic chapters with timestamps when the creator didn't add any.

🎯
Jump to topic

Click a chapter (panel or progress bar) to seek the video.

rocket_launch Get started

1
Click the RecapTube icon and pick a provider — Anthropic Claude or OpenAI GPT.
2
Paste your API key and press Save. Get one from Anthropic or OpenAI.
3
Open any YouTube video with subtitles. The panel appears next to the video with summary and chapters.

lightbulb Good to know

  • RecapTube needs a video that has a transcript/subtitles.
  • Results are cached per video & language for 30 days — reopening is instant and free.
  • Change output language, summary length and toggles anytime in the popup.
  • Exclude channels you don't want processed from the popup.
