RecapTube AI — Help

Summaries, translation & auto-chapters for YouTube

How it works

On a YouTube watch page, RecapTube reads the video's transcript, sends it to your chosen AI provider, and shows a panel next to the video with:

  • Summary + key points, written in your browser's language.
  • Chapters with timestamps — generated by AI only when the creator didn't add their own. Click any chapter (or a marker on the progress bar) to jump there.

Setup

  • Open the popup, choose Claude or OpenAI, paste your API key, press Save.
  • Keys: Anthropic · OpenAI.
  • Pick a model, summary length, and output language (default: auto = browser language).

Troubleshooting

"Transcript not available"

The video has no subtitles/transcript, or YouTube didn't expose it. RecapTube opens the transcript panel automatically to trigger it; try reloading the page.

The summary is in the wrong language

Set Output language in the popup. Auto follows your browser language (navigator.language).

No AI chapters appeared

That's expected when the video already has creator chapters — RecapTube defers to those and won't duplicate the markers. Toggle Auto chapters / Chapter markers in the popup.

Nothing happens

Check the API key is saved (green "Configured"), the master toggle is on, and the channel isn't in your excluded list. Use Regenerate this video to force a fresh run.

Privacy

Your API key is stored locally in the browser and used only to call the provider you selected. Transcripts are sent to that provider to produce the summary/chapters. Results are cached locally per video & language for 30 days.
