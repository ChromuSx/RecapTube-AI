RecapTube AI — Help

Summaries, translation & auto-chapters for YouTube

help_outline How it works

On a YouTube watch page, RecapTube reads the video's transcript, sends it to your chosen AI provider, and shows a panel next to the video with:

  • Summary + key points, written in your browser's language.
  • Chapters with timestamps — AI-generated only when the creator didn't add any. Click a chapter (or a progress-bar marker) to jump there.
  • Ask the video — a Q&A box answered from the transcript, and a translated full transcript.

settings Setup

  • Open the popup, choose Claude or OpenAI, paste your API key, press Save.
  • Keys: Anthropic · OpenAI.
  • Pick a model, summary length, and output language (default: auto = browser language).

build Troubleshooting

"Transcript not available"

The video has no subtitles/transcript, or YouTube didn't expose it. RecapTube opens the transcript panel automatically to trigger it; try reloading the page.

The summary is in the wrong language

Set Output language in the popup. Auto follows your browser language.

No AI chapters appeared

Expected when the video already has creator chapters — RecapTube lists those instead and won't duplicate them. Toggle Auto chapters / Chapter markers in the popup.

Nothing happens

Check the API key is saved, the master toggle is on, and the channel isn't excluded. Use Regenerate to force a fresh run. In manual mode, click Generate recap.

lock Privacy

Your API key is stored locally and used only to call the provider you selected. Transcripts are sent to that provider to produce the summary/chapters. Results are cached locally per video & language for 30 days.
