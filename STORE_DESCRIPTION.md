UNDERSTAND ANY YOUTUBE VIDEO IN SECONDS WITH RECAPTUBE!

Don't have time to watch a 40-minute video? RecapTubeAI reads the video's transcript and instantly gives you a clear summary — in YOUR language — plus auto-generated chapters so you can jump straight to what matters. Powered by your choice of Claude (Anthropic) or OpenAI GPT.


🚀 KEY FEATURES

📝 AI SUMMARY
• A faithful summary plus the key takeaways of any video with subtitles
• Neutral and to-the-point — no fluff
• Choose summary length: short, medium or long

🌍 BUILT-IN TRANSLATION
• The summary is written directly in your browser's language
• Watch a video in any language and read the recap in yours
• Or pick a specific output language in the settings

📑 AUTOMATIC CHAPTERS
• Generates topic chapters with timestamps when the creator didn't add any
• Already has chapters? RecapTube respects them and won't duplicate
• Click a chapter — in the panel or on the progress bar — to jump there instantly

🤖 YOUR CHOICE OF AI
• Claude (Anthropic): Haiku (fast & economical) or Sonnet (most accurate)
• OpenAI: GPT-5.5 (best), GPT-5.4 Mini (fast), GPT-5.4 Nano (cheapest)

🪟 CLEAN IN-PAGE PANEL
• Appears right next to the video — summary, key points and chapters
• Collapsible, with one-click regenerate

🛡️ RESILIENT TO YOUTUBE CHANGES
• Reads YouTube's own transcript data instead of fragile page elements
• If YouTube redesigns its layout, the extension adapts automatically using AI

💾 INTELLIGENT CACHING
• Recaps are saved locally per video AND language for 30 days
• Reopening a video is instant and costs nothing

🔒 PRIVACY & SECURITY
• Use YOUR personal API key (Anthropic or OpenAI)
• All data stored locally on your device
• No tracking, no personal data collection
• Open source


🎬 HOW IT WORKS

1. ONE-TIME SETUP
   • Get an API key from Anthropic (console.anthropic.com) or OpenAI (platform.openai.com/api-keys)
   • Select your provider and paste the key in the extension settings
   • Choose your output language and summary length

2. WHILE WATCHING
   • Open a YouTube video with captions
   • RecapTube reads the transcript and your chosen AI produces the summary + chapters
   • The panel appears next to the video; chapter markers appear on the progress bar
   • Everything is cached locally for next time

3. MANAGEMENT
   • Regenerate or clear a recap anytime from the popup
   • Exclude specific channels you don't want processed


🔧 REQUIREMENTS

• Google Chrome (or a Chromium-based browser)
• An AI API key — Anthropic Claude (console.anthropic.com) or OpenAI (platform.openai.com)
• YouTube videos with available captions/transcripts


💰 API COSTS

The extension uses YOUR own API key, so you pay your provider directly for usage — there is no subscription to us. Thanks to caching, each video+language is processed only ONCE.


🛡️ PRIVACY

WHAT WE COLLECT: NOTHING. No personal data is collected or sent to us.

STORED LOCALLY: your API key, preferences, and the recap cache.

EXTERNAL COMMUNICATIONS:
• Video transcripts → your chosen AI provider (Anthropic or OpenAI)
• Rarely, when YouTube changes its layout, a stripped page-structure snapshot (no personal data) is sent to your provider so the extension can keep reading transcripts
• No other external connections


❓ FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: Does it work on every video?
A: It works on videos that have captions/transcripts. Without a transcript, it can't summarize.

Q: Do I pay to use it?
A: The extension is free. You only pay your AI provider for usage, and caching keeps that to a minimum.

Q: What language is the summary in?
A: Your browser's language by default. You can override it in settings.

Q: What if the video already has chapters?
A: RecapTube uses the creator's chapters and won't generate or duplicate them.

Q: Is my API key secure?
A: Yes — it's stored locally with Chrome's storage API and only sent to the provider you choose.

Q: Does it slow YouTube down?
A: No. Work happens in the background and cached videos load instantly.
